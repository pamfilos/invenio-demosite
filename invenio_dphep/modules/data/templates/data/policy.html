Policy Summary
This policy describes the CMS principles of data preservation, re-use and open access, as well as the relevant actors in all these tasks and their roles and responsibilities. CMS understands that in order to fully exploit all these re-use opportunities, immediate and continued resources are needed. The level of support that CMS will be able to provide to external users depends on the available funding.
